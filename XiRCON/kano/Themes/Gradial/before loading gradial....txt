A. Click Tools, then Preferences, then on the tab labelled Color.
B. Click Load and find the INI you want to load. **
C. Hit OK :) then load the theme if it isn't already.

To make your own fading INI [gradial theme], try /makeini.
Example: /makeini red green christmas.ini
         makes an INI which fades from red to green (saves to xircon\christmas.ini)

To load a color INI, go to Tools->Preferences and click the Colors tab. Press
the "Load" button.

** I suggest starting out with "xircon\kano\Themes\Gradial\Blue Gradial Scheme.ini"
